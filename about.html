Though once cherished possessions, it is possible that you grow out of certain books. Also, what do you do with the hoard of exam materials that you must have spent many nights mugging from to crack that impossible entrance exam? Well, how about donating?

Somaiya Books is a website created for the students of Somaiya Vidyavihar University. It is a simple platform for book donation created by second year IT students of KJSCE under the guidance of Mrs. Anagha Raich ( Faculty, IT Dept., KJSCE). Students can put up their used books for donation as well as get the required prescribed books on the website.


Happy Book-Exchanging!
